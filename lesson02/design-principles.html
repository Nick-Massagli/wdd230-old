Visual Hierarchy

Raycon

  • rayconglobal.com

    • The large image advertising their main product draws the users view, and quickly tells them what the website is for. The use of color contrast also draws attention to where it is most important.

    Hick's Law

    Doordash

  • doordash.com

    • The simplicity in the opening screen having only options on how to log in or sign up gives the user a clear way forward on how to interact with the site.

    PARC: Contrast

    Apple

  • apple.com

    • The black banner and large, bold white text advertising the new iPhone pops out from the iconic stark white that Apple is known for, and lets the user immediately know the important element of the site.
